## Before You Contribute

Ask yourself the following questions:

- [ ] Is my contribution completely useless?
- [ ] Does it solve a problem that does not exist?
- [ ] Would my employer be concerned if they saw this?
- [ ] Have I tried NOT contributing and reconsidered?

If you answered YES to the first three and NO to the last one,
you may proceed.

If your contribution is useful in any way, please close this tab.
We cannot help you here.
