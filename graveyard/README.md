## ⚰️ Graveyard

A resting place for projects that didn't make it.

Abandoned repos, deprecated agents, half-built ideas,
AI pipelines that ran forever and returned nothing.

We do not mourn them. We celebrate them.
They tried. That is enough.
Even AI projects die here. Especially AI projects.
