# 🪦 Graveyard

> Where projects come to rest.

This folder contains projects that were once alive.
They are no longer alive.
We do not speak of why.

Even AI projects die here.
Especially AI projects.
The AI did not see it coming.
The AI never does.

## Residents

_No residents yet._
_It is only a matter of time._

## Admission Requirements

- Must have been abandoned mid-feature
- Must have at least one TODO that became a NEVER
- Must have a node_modules folder that outlived the project
- Commits must end with "fix" but nothing was fixed
- AI agents that ran forever and returned nothing
- Promising demos that never made it to production
- Revolutionary ideas that required just one more iteration
